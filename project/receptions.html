Reception Center

Each of our hotels has a reception center with all of the features to make a wedding reception convenient and affordable. Services include:

  • Seating for up to 200 people
  • A variety of elegant decorations and backdrops are availble for use
  • Experienced staff available to assist behind the scenes
  • Discounts at nearby florists
  • Simple catering with a choice of 4 entrees, appetizers, and a variety of desserts
  • Kitchen access available if you prefer to provide your own food service
  • Discounted group hotel rates are available with some packages
