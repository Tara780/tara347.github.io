Upcoming Events in Preston

Celebrating Napoleon

by Tom Thompson

Preston is a small town nestled in the beautiful Cache Valley. Residents are friendly, and there is a strong sense of community. Each summer, the town hosts a festival celebrating the beloved characters from the movie Napoleon Dynamite, which was filmed in Preston. The movie was released in 2004 and became an instant cult classic. The Napoleon Dynamite festival is one of Preston's most popular events. Each year people come from far and wide to join in the celebration. The festival features tetherball tournaments, a tater-tot-eating contest, a costume contest, and a friendship bracelet station. Concessions and movie-related merchandise are available for purchase. Drop by the park downtown from July 8-12 to join in the fun!
