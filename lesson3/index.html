Site Name

First Watch Weather

Domain Name: FirstWatchWeather.com

Site Purpose

The core purpose of the First Watch Weather website is to be a community resource for residents of Southeastern Idaho and inform users of the local weather conditions. The website should accurately communicate current weather conditions along with a forecast for three different localities (Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven), as well as collect storm information from users. In addition to the weather conditions, additional weather-related content will be provided to keep the user engaged.
