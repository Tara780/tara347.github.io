Target Audience

The target audience of First Watch Weather is anyone from the local community with an interest in local weather. Those with an interest in local weather could include outdoor recreation enthusiasts, those whose jobs require them to work outside, and generally anyone who will be outside during any point throughout the course of the day. The website will target audience members who are seeking information using the internet. They could be accessing the website from a desktop, laptop, tablet, or mobile phone.

Personas

Rick

Rick is a 38 years old and single. He has a bachelor’s degree in business administration and is a project manager at a small advertising agency. He is an outdoor recreation enthusiast and seeks to know what the weather conditions are so that he can plan his weekend activities. Rick uses the computer at work to check the weather.

Susan

Susan is 31 years old. Susan has some college education, but she doesn’t have a degree. She is a stay-at-home mom with two small children—a four-year-old boy named Nate and a two-year-old girl named Jinny. The children love to play outside, so Susan likes to know the weather conditions so she knows how to dress her children. She accesses the site from her phone.

Scenarios

  • Will I need to bring an umbrella?
  • Which days will I need to water my garden this week?
  • Will I need extra time tomorrow morning to warm up my car?
  • What's the forecast for our camping trip this weekend?
  • How should I dress my toddler when we go to the park?
  • Is it hot enough to let my kids play in the sprinklers?
  • Will I need to bring sunblock to the picnic?
